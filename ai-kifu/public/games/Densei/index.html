Densei matches

Each year a match between go-playing computer programs is organized by UEC (University of Electro-Communications, Ch≈çfu, Tokyo, Japan). The winner and runner-up programs then play a 9p human professional (with appropriate handicap) under Japanese rules. The time allotted is 30 min, followed by a byo-yomi of 30 sec/move.

5th Densei-sen

The 10th UEC computer go tournament was held 2017-03-18,19. Winner was FineArt, runner-up DeepZenGo. Their human opponent was Ichiriki Ryo (7p). No handicap was given. The first game was played with 30 min main time and 30 sec/move byo-yomi. The final game with 60 min main time and 60 sec/move byo-yomi. Ichiriki Ryo lost both games. This was the last edition of the series of Densei-sen matches.
