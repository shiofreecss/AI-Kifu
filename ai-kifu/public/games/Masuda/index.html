Masuda Cup

The Masuda Cup (岩本薫記念益田杯 - Iwamoto Kaoru Memorial Masuda Cup) is a memorial event for Iwamoto Kaoru (1902-1999). Participants are 18 years old or younger and insei or 1p or 2p at Nihon Kiin, Tokyo branch. Komi is 6.5. Time allotted is 1h each.
