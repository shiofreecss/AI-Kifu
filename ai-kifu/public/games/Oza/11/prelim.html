11th Oza, preliminaries

1st Preliminary

The 1st preliminary was played in 4 groups between 1-4 dans. The groups were won by Abe Yoshiteru (2p), Honda Teruko (4p), Kanno Kiyonori (1p), and Kurosawa Tadanao (3p).

2nd Preliminary

The four group winners of the 1st Preliminary, together with Hane Yasumasa (3p) and 60 high (i.e., 5-9) dans, played in 15 groups in the 2nd Preliminary.

3rd Preliminary

The 15 group winners of the 2nd Preliminary, together with 12 further players, played in 10 groups in the 3rd Preliminary.
