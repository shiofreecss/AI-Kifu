The NEC Cup

The NEC Cup was a Japanese quick Go tournament, held in 1982-2012.

Participants are the title holders (Kisei, Meijin, Honinbo, Judan, Tengen, Oza, Gosei, NHK Cup, NEC Shun'ei Cup, ...) and top finishers from the previous year.
