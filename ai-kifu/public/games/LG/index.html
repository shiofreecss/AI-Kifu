LG Cup

The LG Cup World King of Baduk Tournament (LG배 세계기왕전, LG杯世界棋王戦, LG盃世界棋王戰) was founded by Korean LG Group in 1996. It is a single knockout tournament, with best-of-five final until the 10th edition, best-of-three afterwards. Komi was 5.5, since the 3rd edition 6.5. Time allotted is 3h.
