Numbers 1 to 5 were straight knockout finals. Since then the winner of the knockout has challenged the holder of the title.

Hane Naoki lost the 3rd game of the 23rd Tengen by an illegal ko capture.

Honorary title

Players who won the Tengen title five or more consecutive times get the Honorary Tengen title. So far these are Rin Kaiho and Iyama Yuta.
