Taiwanese preliminary

The on-line preliminary had 9 Taiwanese participants. The only woman, Lu Yuhua, was selected as winner of the 2023 Omori Cup Women's Masters. Four men, Xu Haohong, Lai Junfu, Wang Yuanjun, Xu Jingen, were selected as title winners in the 2023 Men's Open Professional Go Championship. The remaining four participants were selected from among the remaining eight top money winners. A 1-round preliminary match was held on 2024-02-21. Li Wei, Lin Lixiang, Lin Junyan, Lin Yancheng were beaten by Chen Qirui, Jian Jingting, Xiao Zhenghao, Lu Yiquan, respectively.

North American preliminary

Ryan Li, Alexander Qi, Michael Chen, and Jiang Mingjiu played a preliminary match to determine the two North American participants of the 10th Ing Cup. The winners were Ryan Li and Alexander Qi.
