Ing Cup

The Ing Cup (応氏杯) is a 4-yearly event sponsored by the late Ing Chang-Ki. Games are played according to the Ing Rules, with 8 point komi, complicated rules for types of ko fight, and timing rules where there is no byoyomi but additional time can be bought at the cost of a few points.
