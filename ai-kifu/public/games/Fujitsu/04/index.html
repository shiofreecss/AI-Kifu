Two forfeits

The final between Cho Chikun and Qian Yuping had been scheduled for 1991-08-03, but Qian Yuping had become ill and was unable to come. His clock was started at 10 a.m. and after 30 minutes Cho Chikun was declared the winner.

The third place playoff between Kobayashi Koichi and O Rissei had been scheduled on that same day, but also Kobayashi Koichi had become ill, and O Rissei was declared the winner.
