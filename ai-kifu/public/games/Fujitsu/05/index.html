Participants

The 24 participants of the main tournament are:

  • Top 3 from last year: Cho Chikun, O Rissei (but Qian Yuping was unable to come)
  • 7 from Japan: Fujisawa Shuko, Ishida Yoshio, Kobayashi Koichi, Otake Hideo, Rin Kaiho, Takemiya Masaki, Yoda Norimoto
  • 6 from China: Che Zewu, Liu Xiaoguang, Ma Xiaochun, Nie Weiping, Yu Bin, Zhang Wendong
  • 4 from South Korea: Cho Hunhyun, Lee Changho, Seo Bongsoo, Yoo Changhyuk
  • 2 from Taiwan: Lin Shengxian, Peng Jinghua
  • 1 from Europe: Alexej Lazarev
  • 1 from North America: Michael Redmond
  • 1 from South America: Fernando Aguilar
