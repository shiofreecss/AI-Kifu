South American Preliminary

The South American preliminary was played from 2007-11-04 to 2008-01-12 in two groups. In Group A Eduardo López Herrero (Argentina) and Luis Mariano Cajiao (Costa Rica) ended both with 4 points, and the former won the decision match. Group B was won by Wang Senfeng (Brazil). The final between them was won by Eduardo López Herrero.
