North American Preliminary

The 12th North American Fujitsu Qualifying tournament was held on 1999-01-09,10,11 in Houston, Texas. Places 1 and 2 were for Jimmy Cha and Michael Redmond. Their mutual game, in the 4th and last round, was resigned by Michael Redmond (playing White) after 177 moves.
