JT Cup

The JT Cup (JT Seiza Cup, JT Cup Constellation Go Championship, JT杯星座囲碁選手権戦), was a tournament sponsored by JT Koyosan. It was held five times, in 1995-1999. The main tournament had 32 participants, 6 title holders, 20 from Nihon Ki-in preliminaries, and 6 from Kansai Ki-in. Komi was 5.5. Time 1h for the preliminaries and 2h for the main tournament.

The Nihon Ki-in preliminaries were grouped into 12 groups according to the zodiac sign of the player, see below.
