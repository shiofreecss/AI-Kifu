Preliminaries

In the preliminaries, 24 players (18 from Nihon Ki-in, 6 from Kansai Ki-in) earned access to the main tournament. They were joined by the four semifinalists of last year's main tournament (Murakawa Daisuke, Yamashita Keigo, Sada Atsushi, Sakai Hideyuki), and Ida Atsushi, Takao Shinji (Meijin).
