47th Gosei title

Preliminaries

In the preliminaries, 24 players (18 from Nihon Ki-in, 6 from Kansai Ki-in) earned access to the main tournament. They were joined by the previous Gosei (Ichiriki Ryo) and the three non-winner semifinalists of last year's main tournament (Ida Atsushi, Hane Naoki, Yoda Norimoto), and Shibano Toramaru.
