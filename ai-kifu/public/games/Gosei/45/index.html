45th Gosei title

Preliminaries

In the preliminaries, 24 players (18 from Nihon Ki-in, 6 from Kansai Ki-in) earned access to the main tournament. They were joined by the previous Gosei (Kyo Kagen) and the three non-winner semifinalists of last year's main tournament (Iyama Yuta, Ichiriki Ryo, Yu Zhengqi) and Cho U.
