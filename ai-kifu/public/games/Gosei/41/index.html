41st Gosei title

Preliminaries

In the preliminaries, 24 players earned access to the main tournament.

Nihon Ki-in

18 players won the Nihon Ki-in preliminary A finals, namely Cho Riyu, Goto Shungo, Hane Naoki, Hikosaka Naoto, Ida Atsushi, Kanazawa Makoto, Kim Sujun, Kyo Kagen, Mitani Tetsuya, Mizokami Tomochika, Motoki Katsuya, Numadate Sakiya, Shibano Toramaru, Suzuki Shinji, Tsuruta Kazushi, Tsuruyama Atsushi, Uchida Shuhei, Yamada Shinji.

Kansai Ki-in

6 players won the Kansai Ki-in preliminary A finals, namely Kiyonari Tetsuya, Sada Atsushi, Sakai Hideyuki, Seto Taiki, Yo Seiki = Yu Zhengqi, Yuki Satoshi.

Tournament

There were 29 players, namely the 24 winners of the preliminaries, and the four semifinalists of last year's main tournament (Murakawa Daisuke, Yamashita Keigo, Kono Rin, Shida Tatsuya), and Takao Shinji (Tengen).
