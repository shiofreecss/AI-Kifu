Samsung Cup

The Samsung Cup (Samsung Fire Cup, Samsung Cup World Open Baduk Championship, Samsung Fire & Marine Insurance World Masters Baduk, 삼성화재배, 三星火災杯) is an international tournament where also amateur players are allowed to play.

Komi was 5.5 the first three editions, and 6.5 afterwards.
Time available was 3h for editions 1-9, and 2h afterwards.
