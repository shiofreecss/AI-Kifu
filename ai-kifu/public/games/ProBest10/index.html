Asahi Pro Best Ten title results

The Asahi Pro Best Ten (プロ十傑戦) was a tournament sponsored by Asahi Shimbun and held from 1964 to 1975. Here we give the games and results of the finals. For editions 1-6 the final was a best-of-three, for editions 7-12 a best-of-five match. Komi was 5.5. Time 6h. For details on the separate years, see the links in the first column.
