Kansai Go Open Tournament

The Kansai Go Open Tournament (関西囲碁オープントーナメント) is a tournament between players belonging to the Kansai Branch of Nihon Ki-in, or to the Kansai Ki-in, or are strong amateurs living in Kansai. There are really five separate tournaments with eight participants each, called Top class and Class A-D. The class is determined on the prize ranking.

Komi is 6.5. Time is 3h each (1h in the preliminaries) with 5x 1 min byo-yomi.
