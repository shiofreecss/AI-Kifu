Preliminaries

The participants in the Judan tournament are the 12 players who won at least one game in last year's tournament (except for Sakata Eio, who won the Judan title and was replaced by the former Judan Hashimoto Utaro), together with Go Seigen and the 3 winners of the preliminaries (Kato Masao, Takagi Shoichi, and Kojima Takaho).
