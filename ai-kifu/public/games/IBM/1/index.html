1st IBM Hayago Open

An open and international tournament. The main tournament was preceded by two preliminaries of two rounds each.

Both pros and amateurs participated, but no amateurs reached the main tournament. There was one amateur from Europe: Hans Pietsch, and one from America: Thomas Tu.

The two preliminaries were played on 1988-06-06 and 1988-06-20 (two games on each day).

Main tournament

Sixty-four players competed in the main tournament. Forty survivors from the preliminaries, ten from Nihon Ki-in, ten from Kansai Ki-in, two from China (Chen Linxin and Yang Hui) and two from Korea (Seo Nungwuk and Lee Changho).

The top ten Nihon Ki-in players (ranked by prime money in the preceding year) were seeded, but Sakata withdrew and was replaced by Ishida.
