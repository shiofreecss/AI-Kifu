Go Seigen Cup

The Go Seigen Cup or Wu Qingyuan Cup (吴清源杯世界女子围棋赛, Wu Qingyuan Cup World Women's Weiqi Tournament) is an international Go tournament for women, held annually in Fuzhou, China, the birthplace of Go Seigen.

The rules are Chinese, komi is 7.5. Each player has 2 hours, plus five times one minute.
