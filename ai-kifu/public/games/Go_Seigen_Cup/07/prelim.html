7th Go Seigen Cup, International Preliminary

The 7th Go Seigen Cup World Women's Go Open Integrated Preliminary was held in Fuzhou, China, 2024-09-20..23. There were 102 participants, playing in 10 groups: 79 Chinese, 17 Korean, no Japanese, 5 Taiwanese (Yang Zixuan, Li Jiaxin, Bai Xinhui, Yu Lichun, Lin Yiting), and 1 from Slovakia (Zuzana Kralikova).
