Participants

The main tournament had 24 participants:

Chinese (9): Yu Zhiying 7p (last runner-up), Rui Naiwei 9p, Lu Minquan 6p, Wang Chenxing 5p, Li He 5p, Wang Yubo 4p, Wu Yiming 3p, Li Xiaoxi 2p, Zhou Hongyu 6p (wild card).

Korean (5): Choi Jung 9p (last winner), O Yujin 9p, Kim Chaeyoung 7p, Cho Seunga 5p, Kim Eunji 2p.

Japanese (4): Nyu Eiko 4p, Ueno Asami 4p, Suzuki Ayumi 7p, Nakamura Sumire 2p. Nyu Eiko replaced Fujisawa Rina, who had tested positive for covid-19.

Taiwan (2): Lu Yuhua 4p, Hei Jiajia 7p (wild card).

Europe (2): Dina Burdakova (5d ama), Virzhinia Shalneva (3d ama).

America (2): Feng Yun 9p, Yin Mingming 1p.
