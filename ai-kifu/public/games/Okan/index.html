Okan title games

This page gives the title games (in SGF format) for the Okan (王冠) title.

The Okan title is a 1-game match between the winner of a 12-player knockout tournament and the previous title holder. It is open only to players in the Nagoya branch of the Nihon Ki-in. It is held every year (but up to the 15th Okan, every year-and-a-half).

Komi was 4.5 up to 1974, 5.5 in 1975-2002, and 6.5 from 2003.

For the early years, no game records are available.
