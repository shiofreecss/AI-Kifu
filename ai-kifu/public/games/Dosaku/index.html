Games by Dosaku

Honinbo Dosaku (本因坊道策, 1645–1702) was the fourth Honinbo, the strongest player of his times. His real name was Yamazaki Sanjiro. He succeeded his teacher, the 3rd Honinbo Doetsu, in 1677, and was appointed Meijin Godokoro the next year. His younger brother Dosa had become Inoue Dosa Inseki in 1672.

This page gives the 153 games that have been preserved. Various sources have versions that differ in details.

The name "Honinbo Dosaku" is used everywhere below (and not, e.g., Yamazaki).
