Honorary title

Players who won the Honinbo title five or more consecutive times get a honorary Honinbo title. So far these are

Honorary title Name

23rd Honinbo Sakata Eio
24th Honinbo Ishida Yoshio
25th Honinbo Cho Chikun
26th Honinbo Iyama Yuta
