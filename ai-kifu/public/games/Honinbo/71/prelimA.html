Preliminary A of the 71st Honinbo tournament

For several big Japanese tournaments, players progress via Preliminary C and Preliminary B and Preliminary A and Last Preliminary to the League that determines the challenger of the title holder. The whole process takes almost two years.

The present page gives the (Nihon Ki-in) Preliminary A of the 71st Honinbo tournament. In a knock-out system, 108 players compete in 28 groups of 4 (or 3) for a place in the Last preliminary.
