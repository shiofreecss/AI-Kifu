Preliminary A of the 77th Honinbo tournament

For several big Japanese tournaments, players progress via Preliminary C and Preliminary B and Preliminary A and Last Preliminary to the League that determines the challenger of the title holder. The whole process takes almost two years.

The present page gives the Preliminary A of the 77th Honinbo tournament. In a knock-out system, 96 players from the Nihon Ki-in compete in 24 groups of 4 and 32 players from the Kansai Ki-in compete in 4 groups of 8 for a place in the Last preliminary.
