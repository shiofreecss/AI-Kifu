14th Japan-China Go Exchange, 1986

Go World 44, 46 and 48 write:
In the 14th Japan-China Go Exchange, a Chinese team toured Japan from 27 April to 18 May and scored 31 wins to 25 losses in seven matches against Japanese teams.

The cumulative score for the fourteen tours to date is 385 wins to 370 losses for Japan (plus 20 jigos and 2 no-results). The first series was won 40-14-2 jigos by Japan, but at this rate it will only be a couple of years before the Chinese take the lead.
