16th Japan-China Go Exchange, 1988

Go World 52 writes:
This year a Chinese team toured Japan in April in the 16th Japan-China Go Exchange. The final score after seven team matches was 39-17 in China's favour. This is China's best result since 1982, when it scored 43-13 (including 13 wins against amateurs).
