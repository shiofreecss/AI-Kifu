5th Daiwa Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open

Preliminaries

The preliminaries were played on 2009-04-27 and 2009-05-11 between twenty groups of 3 players, 60 players in all. Each group of 3 players played a knockout tournament (of 2 games).

Tournament

The 20 winners from the preliminaries and the eight players Cho Chikun, Cho U, Kono Rin, Yoda Norimoto, O Rissei, Shuto Shun, Kobayashi Koichi, Komatsu Hideki that reached the second round of the 4th Daiwa Cup Tournament play a knockout tournament. The four players that reached the third round in that tournament get a bye.
