4th Daiwa Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open

Preliminaries

The preliminaries were played on 2008-05-19,20,26 between twenty groups of 3 players, 60 players in all. Each group of 3 players played a knockout tournament (of 2 games).

Tournament

The 20 winners from the preliminaries and the eight players Cho Chikun, Rin Kanketsu, Kim Sujun, Iyama Yuta, Ryu Shikun, Takao Shinji, Yamada Kimio, Komatsu Hideki that reached the second round of the 3rd Daiwa Cup Tournament play a knockout tournament. The four players that reached the third round in that tournament get a bye.
