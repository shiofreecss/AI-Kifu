8th Daiwa Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open

Preliminaries

The preliminaries were played on 2012-06-11, 06-25, 07-09, 07-16 and 08-27 between 24 groups of 4 players, 96 players in all. Each group of 4 players played a knockout tournament (of 3 games).

Tournament

The 24 winners of the preliminaries, together with the 8 players that reached the second round of the 7th Daiwa Cup Tournament, play a knockout tournament.
