3rd Daiwa Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open

Preliminaries

The preliminaries were played on 2007-08-07, 14, 21, 28 between twenty groups of 3 players and two groups of 2 players, 64 players in all. Each group of 2 or 3 players played a knockout tournament (of 1 or 2 games).

Tournament

The 22 winners from the preliminaries and the 7 participants other than Cho U in the 2nd Daiwa Tournament (namely Yoda Norimoto (Honinbo), Takao Shinji (Meijin), O Meien (Daiwa Cup), Kono Rin (Tengen), Cho Chikun (Judan), Iyama Yuta, Kim Sujun) play a knockout tournament. The three players that reached the second round of the 2nd Daiwa Tournament get a bye.
