Participants

The 32 participants of the main tournament are:

  • 10 from Japan: Cho Sonjin, Cho U, Hane Naoki, Hikosaka Naoto, Rin Kaiho, Ryu Shikun, Seto Taiki, Takao Shinji, Yoda Norimoto, Yuki Satoshi
  • 8 from China: Chang Hao, Chen Yaoye, Choi Cheolhan, Gu Li, Kong Jie, Luo Xihe, Peng Quan, Zhou Heyang
  • 6 from Korea: Cho Hanseung, Ko Geuntae, Lee Changho, Lee Sedol, Park Jungsang, Park Yeonghun
  • 1 from Taiwan: Zhou Junxun
  • 3 from Europe: Alexander Dinerchtein (Russia), Guo Juan (The Netherlands), Ilya Shikshin (Russia)
  • 2 from North America: Feng Yun, Jiang Mingjiu
  • 1 from South America: Fernando Aguilar (Argentina)
  • 1 from the rest of the world: Yang Shihai (Hong Kong)

On Oct 7-9, 2005, the 7th Latin American Go Championship was held. This also served as the South American Preliminary for the 3rd Toyota & Denso Cup. It was won by Fernando Aguilar.
