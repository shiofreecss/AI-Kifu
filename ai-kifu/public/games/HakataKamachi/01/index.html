1st Hakata Kamachi Cup

The Hakata Kamachi Cup (博多・カマチ杯) was a new tournament for women, held only once, in 2020. It was won by Fujisawa Rina.

After the first edition, it was renamed Hakata Kamachi Cup Women’s Meijin Tournament to provide the continuation for the discontinued Women’s Meijin Tournament (that also had Fujisawa Rina as the current title holder) starting with the 32nd edition.

Komi was 6.5. Time 2h, in the preliminaries 1h.
