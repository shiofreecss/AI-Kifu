1st Tri Cup

Five 3-player teams play a full tournament, consisting of five rounds. In each round four teams play, one team has a bye. A match between two teams consists of three games between the members of the opposite teams. The team captain determines the order.

The teams

A. Miori society: Shimosaka Miori 2p, Cho Riyu 8p, Rin Kanketsu 7p

B. Darkhorse: Nyu Eiko 1p, Tsuruta Kazushi 4p, Mutsuura Yuta 3p

C. Ichi go: Mannami Nao 3p, Yamamoto Kentaro 5p, Shimojima Yohei 8p

D. Xie family: Motoki Katsuya 8p, Hirata Tomoya 7p, Xie Yimin 3crown

E. Miracle: Muramatsu Hiroki 6p, Miyamoto Chiharu 1p, Kikkawa Hajime 2p

The matching

Round 1: A beats B 2-1, D beats C 3-0.

Round 2: A beats C 3-0, B beats E 2-1.

Round 3: D beats A 3-0, E beats C 2-1.

Round 4: A beats E 2-1, D beats B 2-1.

Round 5: C beats B 2-1, D beats E 3-0.

Final standing: D 4-0, A 3-1, B, C, E 1-3. Xie family wins.
