Teikei Cups

In 2021, Teikei Co., Ltd. started sponsoring three Go events, here called the Teikei Shunei Cup (テイケイ杯俊英戦), the Teikei Legend Cup (テイケイ杯レジェンド戦), and the Teikei Women's Legend Cup (テイケイ杯女流レジェンド戦).
