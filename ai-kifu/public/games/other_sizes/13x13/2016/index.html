1st 13x13 Pro-Ama Tournament

The 1st 13x13 Pro-Ama Tournament was held in July 2016, with final on 2016-07-23, and preliminaries on 2016-05-31. The 16 participants of the main tournament were the seeded players Ishida Yoshio and Takao Shinji, and the 8 winners of the preliminaries (see below), and the players Takemiya Masaki, Kono Rin, Muramatsu Ryuichi and Suzuki Ayumi chosen by popular vote, and the amateurs Shibano Ryunosuke (61st amateur Honinbo) and Yamada Masanari.

Komi was 6.5. Time allotted was 30s/move plus 10 x 1 min.
