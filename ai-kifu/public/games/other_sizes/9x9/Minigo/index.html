Minigo

From April 1987 to June 2002 every Sunday morning a 9x9 go match was broadcast on Yomiuri TV in Osaka. It was called Mini-Go 1-Game Match (ミニ碁一番勝負). Komi was 5.5 up to 1995. From 1996 on, komi was 6.5. The time available was a "sudden death" 2 min 30 secs (which explains the rather common loss on time).

The system has varied. In the first few years it was a win-and-continue system until a player had won five consecutive times. Below a player name is listed in purple on the 5th win.

Below we list the matches, and link to the actual game where available.
All dates are broadcast dates, both below and in the game records.
