2nd Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Ichiriki Ryo (7p), Yu Zhengqi (7p), Motoki Katsuya (3p), Fujimura Yosuke (2p), Sada Atsushi (2p), Koyama Kuya (2p).
Korea: Na Hyun (6p), Lee Donghun (5p), Shin Jinseo (3p).
China: Huang Yunsong (4p), Yang Dingxin (3p), Li Qincheng (1p).
Taiwan: Lin Junyan (6p).
Europe: Pavol Lisy (1p).
America: Lionel Zhang (7d ama).
Asia/Oceania: Krit Jamkachornkiat (7d ama).

Japanese preliminary

There were six Japanese players. Last year's winner was seeded. The other five were the winners of five groups of preliminary games.
