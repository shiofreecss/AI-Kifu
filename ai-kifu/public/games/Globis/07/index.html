7th Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Hirose Yuichi (4p), Otake Yu (4p), Ueno Asami (3p), Seki Kotaro (3p), Yahata Naoki (2p), Tanaka Koyu (2p).
Korea: Park Sangjin (4p), Park Joonhoon (4p), Moon Minjong (2p).
China: Li Weiqing (8p), Xie Ke (8p), Liao Yuanhe (8p).
Taiwan: Lai Junfu (5p)
Europe: Anton Chernykh (6d ama).
America: Evan Lin (ama).
Asia/Oceania: Chuan Gao (ama).

Japanese preliminary

The six Japanese players were the winners of six groups of preliminary games.
