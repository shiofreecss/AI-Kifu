5th Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Mutsuura Yuta (7p), Shibano Toramaru (7p), Yo Chito (Yao Zhi Teng, 4p), Fujisawa Rina (3p), Onishi Ryuhei (3p), and Seki Kotaro (1p).
Korea: Lee Donghun (9p), Shin Jinseo (8p), and Shin Minjun (7p).
China: Xu Jiayang (7p), Zhao Chenyu (7p), Xie Ke (5p).
Taiwan: Xu Haohong (5p).
Europe: Anton Chernykh (7d ama).
America: Melissa Cao (Cao Xintian, 5d ama).
Asia/Oceania: Chang Fukang (7d ama).

Japanese preliminary

The six Japanese players were the winners of six groups of preliminary games.
