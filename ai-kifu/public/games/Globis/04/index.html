4th Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Ichiriki Ryo (7p), Kyo Kagen (4p), Yo Chito (Yao Zhi Teng, 4p), Mutsuura Yuta (3p), Shibano Toramaru (3p), and Cho Zuiketsu (Chang Ruei Jie, 2p).
Korea: Shin Jinseo (6p), Byun Sangil (5p), and Shin Minjun (5p).
China: Huang Yunsong (6p), Gu Zihao (5p), Xie Erhao (4p).
Taiwan: Jian Jingting (2p).
Europe: Viacheslav Kaymin (7d ama).
America: Willis Huang (7d ama).
Asia/Oceania: Jie Hui Kwa (7d ama).

Japanese preliminary

The six Japanese players were the winners of six groups of preliminary games.
