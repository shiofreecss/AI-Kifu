Shinjin-O finals

The Shinjin-O tournament (新人王, King of the New Stars) is organized by Nihon Ki-in since 1976. It is a knock-out tournament for players under 7p and less than 30 years (since 2006: less than 25 years) old. The final is a match over 3 games.
