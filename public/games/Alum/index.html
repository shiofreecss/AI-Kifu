Hiroshima-Aluminum Cup

The Hiroshima-Aluminum Cup (広島アルミ杯) or Young Carp fight (若鯉戦 - wakagoi-sen) is a go tournament for young pros, first held in 2006.

Time is 30 sec/move, with 10 × 1 min. Komi is 6.5.
