Shusaku Numbers

Some go players like to find their Shusaku number, that is the length of the shortest chain joining them to Shusaku, where subsequent players in the chain have played go together.

Shusaku

Shusaku was the strongest go player of his time. He won each of the 19 Castle Games he played.

Shusaku used various names. He was born as Kuwahara Torajiro (1829-06-06), used Yasuda Eisai 1835-1841, Yasuda Shusaku 1841-1848 (after reaching 2d), Kuwahara Shusaku 1848, Honinbo Shusaku 1848-1862. He died 1862-09-03.

Rank promotion: 1d: 1840-01-03, 2d: 1841-10-30, 3d: 1842-08-15, 4d: 1843-11-27, 5d: 1846-12, 6d: 1848, 7d: 1853.
