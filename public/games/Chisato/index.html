Chisato Cup

The Chisato Cup (25周年記念囲碁千里杯) was a knockout tournament held only once, in 2014.
Participants were pro players not older than 25 years.
The time limit was 1h30m for each player. Komi was 6.5.

Winner was Suzuki Shinji (23).
