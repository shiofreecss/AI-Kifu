Ishida won 4-2.

There is a very detailed commentary on this title match, The 1971 Honinbo Tournament, by Kaoro Iwamoto (and James C. Davies), Ishi Press, 1976. It contains an analysis of these six games, the seven preceding League games by Ishida, and his last game in the Preliminaries.
