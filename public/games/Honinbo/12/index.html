12th Honinbo title

Preliminaries

Unlike last year, the preliminaries again follow a knockout system. The finals of the preliminaries were won by Suzuki Etsuo, Hashimoto Utaro, Murashima Yoshinori, and Yamabe Toshiro.

League

From now on, the League has 8 players. Each year the bottom four are demoted. Each year the four winners of the preliminaries are added.
