1st Tianfu Cup

The Tianfu Cup (天府杯世界囲碁プロ選手権戦, Tianfu Cup World Professional Go Championship) was held only once, in 2018, in China, under Chinese rules.

The 32 players played two knockout tournaments of 16 players each, and the top 2 from each group played the 4-person main tournament with a best-of-3 final. The winner was Chen Yaoye.

Participants

From China (13): Gu Li (9p), Chen Yaoye (9p), Tan Xiao (9p), Jiang Weijie (9p), Tang Weixing (9p), Lian Xiao (9p), Dang Yifei (9p), Fan Tingyu (9p), Ke Jie (9p), Gu Zihao (9p), Xie Erhao (9p), Peng Liyao (6p), Liao Yuanhe (6p)

From Korea (7): Lee Changho (9p), Choi Cheolhan (9p), Kang Dongyun (9p), Kim Jiseok (9p), Park Junghwan (9p), Lee Donghun (9p), Shin Jinseo (9p)

From Japan (6): Yamashita Keigo (9p), Murakawa Daisuke (8p), Ichiriki Ryo (8p), Motoki Katsuya (8p), Kyo Kagen (8p), Yu Zhengqi (7p)

From Taiwan (2): Wang Yuanjun (8p), Lin Junyan (7p)

From North America (2): Jiang Mingjiu (7p), Lu Haojun (1p)

From Europe (2): Ilya Shikshin (2p), Ali Jabarin (2p).
