2nd IBM Hayago Open

Main tournament

An open and international tournament. The main tournament was preceded by two preliminaries of two rounds each.

Both pros and amateurs participated, but no amateurs reached the main tournament. There was one amateur from Europe: Frederic Donzet, and one from America: Joseph Wang.

The two preliminaries were played on 1989-05-29 and 1989-06-26 (two games on each day).
