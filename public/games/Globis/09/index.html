9th Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Tsuji Shigehito (3p), Fukuoka Kotaro (3p), Watanabe Kandai (2p), Nishimura Jin (1p), Jo Bunen (1p), Mori Chisaki (1p)

Taiwan: Lai Junfu (7p)
Europe: Arved Pittner (ama)
America: Kevin Yang (ama)
