6th Globis Cup

Players are not older than 20. Six from Japan, three from Korea, three from China, one from Taipei, one from Europe, one from America, one from Oceania.

Participants

Japan: Mutsuura Yuta (7p), Shibano Toramaru (7p), Onishi Ryuhei (4p), Hirose Yuichi (3p), Ueno Asami (2p), Nyu Eiko (2p).
Korea: Shin Jinseo (9p), Shin Minjun (9p), Park Sangjin (4p).
China: Chen Zijian (7p), Wang Zejin (6p), Ding Hao (6p).
Taiwan: Chen Qirui (5p).
Europe: Anton Chernykh (7d ama).
America: Brandon Zhou (Zhou Yicheng, 6d ama)
Asia/Oceania: Pongsakarn Sornarra (7d ama)

Japanese preliminary

The six Japanese players were the winners of six groups of preliminary games.

Group stage

The sixteen participants are divided into four groups of four. Each group contributes two players to the main tournament.
