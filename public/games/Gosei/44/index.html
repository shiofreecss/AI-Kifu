Preliminaries

In the preliminaries, 24 players (18 from Nihon Ki-in, 6 from Kansai Ki-in) earned access to the main tournament. They were joined by the previous Gosei (Iyama Yuta) and the three non-winner semifinalists of last year's main tournament (Motoki Katsuya, Seto Taiki, Takao Shinji).
