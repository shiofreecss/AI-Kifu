15th Japan-China Go Exchange, 1987

Go World 48 writes:
In late May and early June this year an eight-man Japanese team headed by Kato Masao toured China as the 15th in the series of annual goodwill tours. The Japanese team won 32 of the 56 games they played, making this the most successful visit to China since the first tour in 1973 (when they won 40 games).

The meetings were in Beijing (5/20, 5/22, 5/24), Chongqing (5/26), Yangtze (5/28, 5/31) and Wuhan (6/3).
