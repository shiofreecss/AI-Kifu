Ryusei matches

The Ryusei Tournament (竜星戦, 龍星戦) is a lightning go tournament consisting of four sections of 24 players each (in 1996, 1997 of 12 players each), after 2001 of eight sections of 12 players each, each run as a "win and continue" tournament. The winner of each section, and the player having won the most games in each section, play in a single knockout tournament to determine the winner.

We give links to subpages for the yearly Ryusei tournaments, and the final.
