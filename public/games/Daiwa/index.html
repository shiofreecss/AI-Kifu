Daiwa-Shoken Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open (大和証券杯ネット囲碁オープン)

The Daiwa Cup is sponsored by Daiwa Securities Group.
Komi is 6.5. Time is 10x1m plus 30s/move byo-yomi.
