1st Daiwa Cup

Daiwa Cup = Daiwa-Shoken Cup = Daiwa Securities Cup Net Igo Open

The Daiwa Cup is sponsored by Daiwa Securities Group. The time allowance is five minutes per player, followed by a byo-yomi of 30 seconds per move.

The format of the tournament varies. This first time a round robin with 4 leagues and 4 players in each league, followed by a somewhat irregular tournament. The final is here a best-of-three.

Tournament

Five title holders and four winners of the four leagues play a knockout tournament. The final is a best-of-three.

Title holders: Takao Shinji (Honinbo), Kono Rin (Tengen), Cho U (Meijin), Cho Chikun (Judan), Yoda Norimoto (ex-Gosei).
