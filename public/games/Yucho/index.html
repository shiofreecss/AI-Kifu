Yucho Cup

The Yucho Cup (ゆうちょ杯　囲碁ユース選手権, Post Cup Go Youth Championship) was started in 2014, on the occasion of the 90th anniversary of Nihon Ki-in. The Yucho Bank (postal savings bank) is the main sponsor. Participants are pros and inseis not older than 20 and not stronger than 7p. The tournament was discontinued after the third edition.
