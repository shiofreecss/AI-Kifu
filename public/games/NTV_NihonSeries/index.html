NTV Nihon Series

The Nihon Series, sponsored by NTV, was a league between four (in 1973) or five (in 1975) strong players. It was held twice.

1st NTV Nihon Series

Held Fall 1973.

League

# player result 1 2 3 4
1. Ishida Yoshio 2-1 W+1.5
2. Sakata Eio 2-1 W+4.5 W+R
3. Rin Kaiho 1-2 W+16.5
4. Otake Hideo 1-2 W+R B+R

Playoff

Both Ishida Yoshio and Sakata Eio had two wins. In the playoff, Ishida won.
