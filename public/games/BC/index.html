BC Card Cup

The BC Card Cup World Baduk Championship is an international Go competition sponsored by South Korea's BC Card credit card company. It was held four times, in 2009-2012. Its predecessor was the Korean BC Card Cup.

Komi is 6.5. Time was 1h each in 2009, 2h each from 2010. The tournament is a knockout tournament, with a best-of-five final.
