1984 - 4th Fujisawa tour

Nine Japanese players toured China and played various opponents. Go World 36 writes:
This year, for the fourth year in a row, Fujisawa Shuko made a private tour of China, taking with him a group of promising young professionals. ... The group this year consisted of nine young professionals, including Kiyonari Tetsuya 8-dan (age 23), Yoda Norimoto 5-dan (age 18) and Shuko's son, Kazunari 2-dan (age 19). ... The team played four matches against Chinese teams, including one all-woman team, and scored 16 wins to 20 losses.

1987

Go World 49 writes:
Every year Fujisawa Shuko makes a private tour of China. In March this year he took 19 young Japanese professionals with him, making his biggest group so far. They played five goodwill matches with Chinese players and managed to come out on top with 51 wins to 43 losses and l jigo.
