Participants

The 16 participants of the main tournament are:

  • 5 from the Nihon Ki-in: Cho Chikun, Kato Masao, Kobayashi Koichi, Rin Kaiho, Takemiya Masaki
  • 2 from the Kansai Ki-in: Imamura Toshiya, Shiraishi Yutaka
  • 4 from the Chinese Weiqi Association: Cao Dayuan, Ma Xiaochun, Nie Weiping, Yu Bin
  • 3 from the Korea Baduk association (Han Kuk Kiwon): Chang Doojin, Cho Hunhyun, Seo Bongsoo
  • 1 from the European Go Federation: Ronald Schlemper
  • 1 from the American Go Association: Jungho Lim
In this 1st edition, players represent their go association, rather than their country. (In particular the Nihon Ki-in members Michael Redmond and James Kerwin, and the Han Kuk Kiwon member Janice Kim, could not play for the AGA.) This changed starting from the 2nd edition.
