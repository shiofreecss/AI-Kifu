China-Japan Ryusei Match

In 2014 and 2016-2018 a 1-game match was held between the winners of the Chinese Longxing and Japanese Ryusei titles.

The rules of the host country are followed. In particular, komi is 6.5 points in Japan, and 7.5 in China.
