World Mingren

World Mingren, or Super Meijin, or China-Japan-Korea Meijin is a tournament between the three winners of the Mingren tournament in China, the Meijin tournament in Japan, and the Myeongin tournament in Korea. Each year there are three games. The first between two of the three players. The second between the loser of the first game and the third player. The final between the winner of the first game and the winner of the second game. The World Mingren was held five times, in 2010, 2011, 2012, 2015, and 2018, each time in China. The rules are Chinese, komi is 7.5.
