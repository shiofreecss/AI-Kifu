Kirin Cup

The Kirin Cup (キリン杯), sponsored by Kirin Brewery, is a knockout tournament between twelve teams of five (in the first two editions) or seven players. It was played nine times, from 1988 to 1996. Each team consisted of three professional players and two, later four, amateurs. The 7th player was a woman. Komi is 5.5, time 30 min each. The Kido Yearbooks give full information on all teams. Here we only show the finalists. See also Wikipedia.
