Participants

The 32 participants of the main tournament are:

  • 10 from Japan: Cho U, Hikosaka Naoto, Kobayashi Koichi, Mizokami Tomochika, Sakai Hideyuki, So Yokoku, Takao Shinji, Yamashita Keigo, Yoda Norimoto, Yuki Satoshi
  • 8 from China: Chang Hao, Gu Li, Kong Jie, Peng Quan, Wang Lei, Wang Xi, Yu Bin, Zhou Heyang
  • 6 from Korea: Cho Hunhyun, Choi Cheolhan, Lee Changho, Lee Sedol, Song Taekon, Yoo Changhyuk
  • 1 from Taiwan: Zhou Junxun
  • 3 from Europe: Alexander Dinerchtein (Russia), Andrey Kulkov (Russia), Frank Janssen (The Netherlands)
  • 2 from North America: Feng Yun, Jiang Mingjiu
  • 1 from South America: Fernando Aguilar (Argentina)
  • 1 from the rest of the world: Yang Jinhua (Singapore)

North American Preliminary

The North American Oza was held in Seattle and New York on Jan 15-18, 2004. Winners were Feng Yun in the East, and Jiang Mingjiu in the West.

South American Preliminary

On Sep 15-21, 2003, the 5th Latin American Go Championship was held. This also served as the South American Preliminary for the 2nd Toyota & Denso Cup. It was won by Fernando Aguilar (Argentina).
