Toyota & Denso Cup

The Toyota & Denso Cup World Go Oza (トヨタ&デンソー杯囲碁世界王座戦) was an international Go tournament held four times in 2002-2009. It was sponsored by the Toyota and Denso corporations.

Participants are 10 professionals from Japan, 5 from China, 5 from Korea, 7 invited players, and the winners of preliminaries held in Europe, North America, South America, and in Asia-Oceania-Africa.

Time is 3h each, komi 5.5 the first edition, 6.5 afterwards. The final was a single game the first edition, a best-of-three afterwards.
