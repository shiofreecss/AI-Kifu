Chubu Saikoi Title

There have been three editions of the Chubu Saikoi Title tournament (中部最高位戦), all won by Iwata Tatsuaki. Komi was 5.5, time 6h.

# date winner loser result
1 1976 Iwata Tatsuaki Baba Shigeru 2-0
2 1977 Iwata Tatsuaki Shimamura Toshihiro 2-1
3 1978 Iwata Tatsuaki Tsuchida Masamitsu 2-1

For the 2nd edition the link in the first column gives the full tournament table (and two final games).
