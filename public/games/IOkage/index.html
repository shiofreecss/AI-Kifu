International Okage Cup

The International Okage Cup (おかげ杯国際新鋭対抗戦, Okage = `Thanks') is a match between four 5-person teams (3 men, 2 women) from China, Korea, Japan and Taiwan. The first edition had a slightly different setup, with 3-person teams (2 men, 1 woman).
