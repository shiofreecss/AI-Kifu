Y's Academy Cup

Y's Academy Cup (ワイズアカデミー杯) is an 8-player league.
Participants are 4 young pros and 4 young amateurs.
In the pro-vs-ama games the amateur takes black, and komi is −5.5 (for editions 1, 2), or 0 (for editions 3, 4) with a 2-stone handicap. In the remaining games komi is 6.5.
Time is 30 min each, with 5 min byoyomi.
