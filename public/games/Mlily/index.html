Mlily Cup

The Mlily Cup (梦百合杯, in full 梦百合杯世界围棋公开赛 Dream Lily Cup World Go Open Tournament) is an international Go tournament held in China every two years. Rules are Chinese, komi is 7.5. There is a 2h time limit, except in the finals where the limit is 3h.

The main tournament has 64 players, 14 of whom are seeded (5 Chinese, 3 Japanese, 3 Korean, 1 Taiwanese, and the winner and runner-up of the previous edition). Participants play following a single-knockout system, except that semifinals are best-of-3 and finals best-of-5.
