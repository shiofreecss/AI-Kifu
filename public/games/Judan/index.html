Judan title games

This page gives the games (in SGF format) from the Judan (十段) title match.
For results from the tournament, see the subpages linked to (in the first column).

Up to the 49th Judan, the tournament had 16 participants, namely (in early years) the 12 players with at least one win in the previous tournament, plus 4 winners from the preliminaries, or (in later years) the 8 players with at least two wins in the previous tournament, plus 8 winners from the preliminaries.

Honorary title

Players who won the Judan title five or more consecutive times get the Honorary Judan title. So far this has not happened.
