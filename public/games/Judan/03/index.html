Preliminaries

The participants in this Judan tournament were the 11 players who won at least one game in last year's tournament (other than the winner Handa Dogen), the previous title holder Hashimoto Utaro, the 3 winners of the preliminaries (Sato Sunao, Yamabe Toshiro, Kada Katsuji), and finally Fujisawa Shuko.
